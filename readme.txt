===============================================================================
= Controls
===============================================================================

Alt+Enter                              Toggle fullscreen

WASD                                   Pan camera

1-2 (no tower selected)                Construct wall and recruit, respectively.
                                       Holding shift allows you to place multiple.

1-3 (tower selected)                   Upgrade selected tower to upgrade in slot 1-3

4 (tower selected)                     Sell selected tower

Space                                  Toggle pause

Q                                      Toggle double time

G                                      Call wave

R                                      Enter demolish mode
